About us

Founded in 2016 by Alice and Norman, Maxx Café was built on their love for cats.

As Bath’s only cat café, their mission is to show others what cats are all about and why they are fantastic companions.

The café is home to four rescue cats that love having visitors and can usually be found playing with guests or snuggling in our cozy booths.

Our Team

Our four resident cats work every day in Maxx Cafe, entertaining guests and lapping up attention. They take regular breaks to keep them happy, so don’t be surprised if you spot members of our team sneaking in a cat nap at your table!
