About us

Founded in 2016 by Alice and Norman, Maxx Cafe was built on their love for cats. As Bath’s only cat cafe, their mission is to show others what cats are all about and why they are fantastic companions. The cafe is home to four rescue cats that love having visitors and can usually be found playing with guests or snuggling in our cozy booths.

Our Team

Workshop

Cat Cupcakes

Come and join us to learn how to make these adorable and delicious cat cupcakes, sure to feed your kitty addiction! No need to bring any ingredients; we provide the kit and the kitties to make sure you get the most out of your experience!

Cat Toys

Let’s get crafty in this cat toy workshop, where you’ll learn to make your furry friend’s favourite toys. All resources are supplied by us, so you can relax and enjoy getting creative. You can take your creations home for your own cat or put them to the test with ours!
